# Students-Complaint-Portal
The Students Complaint Portal is a web-based system that allows students to submit complaints about academics, faculty, or campus facilities. Students can submit complaints anonymously or with their details, track the status of their complaints, and receive updates until issues are resolved. The portal helps the administration manage complaints efficiently and ensures transparency and accountability.

Features:

Complaint submission (anonymous or named)

Status tracking of complaints

Notifications and updates

Admin dashboard to view and respond

Pros:

Easy communication between students and administration

Faster resolution of issues

Transparency and accountability

Organized record of all complaints

Technologies Used:

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Backend: PHP

Database: MySQL
Bootstrap for UI, Email notifications

It improves campus management by addressing problems in a timely and efficient manner.
